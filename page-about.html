About Us

Landmark UK Properties Ltd, established in 2018 (Company Registration Number: 11621643), is a UK-based property investment and development company specialising in the acquisition, purchase, and transformation of residential and commercial assets.

With over 20+ years of combined industry experience, our team has built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality development projects, value-driven asset optimisation, and strategic property investment solutions across the United Kingdom.

Our Journey & Company History

Since 2018, Landmark UK Properties Ltd has focussed on identifying underperforming, outdated, or vacant commercial assets and transforming them into modern, efficient, high-demand residential units. Our approach blends strategic acquisition, modern architectural redesign, regulatory compliance, and strong project management to deliver long-term growth and investment stability.

Over the years, we have grown into a trusted partner for developers, investors, property owners, and institutions seeking reliable and scalable development solutions.

Our Expertise

  • Commercial & residential property investment
  • Fast and discreet property purchases
  • Commercial office block to residential conversion
  • Full-cycle property development (Acquisition → Design → Build → Delivery)
  • Planning, building regulations & fire safety compliance
  • Project and construction management
  • Investor portfolio growth & long-term value strategies

Our Mission

To create sustainable value for investors, partners, and communities by transforming commercial and residential properties into high-quality, efficient, and desirable living spaces that meet modern standards of comfort and safety.

Our Vision

To be recognised as a trusted, forward-thinking property investment and development company delivering innovative, cost-efficient, and high-value solutions across the UK’s residential property market.
